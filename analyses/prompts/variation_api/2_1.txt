This study will perform a weighted comparison between groups with and without diabetic retinopathy in the worse eye.The analysis will use NHANES data from the 2005-2008 period. 
The study includes participants whoes age in years is 40 and older who have diabetes, defined as either doctor told you have diabetes or a glycated hemoglobin (HbA1c) level of 6.5 or higher. 
Individuals with missing data for race/Hispanic origin, age, gender, or any diabetic retinopathy gradings from the worse eye will be excluded.
The analysis will consider several variables, including age in years, systolic blood pressure 1st (mmHg), diastolic blood pressure 1st (mmHg), glycohemoglobin HbA1C (%).
The primary grouping variable is the presence of any retinopathy level, worse eye, which is categorized as either no retinopathy (o) or any retinopathy (1).
