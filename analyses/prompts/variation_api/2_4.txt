Using NHANES data from the years 2005 to 2008, we will do a weigted logistic regression using exam weights.
Participants who don't have missing values for four level retinopathy grading will be included.
Participants who have diabetes will be included when diabetes was defined as meeting any of the following criteria: self-reported diabetes, use of insulin, use of diabetes medication, glycohemoglobin A1c level (%) >= 6.5%, oral glucose tolerance test (mmol/L) >= 11.1mmol/L, or serum glucose (mmol/L) >=7.0mmol/L.
Participants missing hdl-cholestrol will be excluded. The main predictor is hdl-cholestrol, categorized in tertiles(%) T1: ≤33, T2: >33 and ≤66, T3: >66. Adjust the analysis for race/Hispanic origin, age at screening, and gender.
The outcome is retinopathy presence, based on the four level retinopathy grading.
