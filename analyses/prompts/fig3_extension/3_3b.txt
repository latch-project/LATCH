We want to do a logistic regression analysis to study the association between UACR-SACR ratio and diabetic retinopathy. 
UACR-SACR ratio will be calculated as ((urinary albumin (ug/ml) divided by urinary creatinine (ug/ml)*1000)/ (serum albumin ALB (g/dL)/serum creatinine SCR (mg/dL))) and then categorized in quartiles in quartiles Q1: <=25%, Q2: >25 and <=50, Q3: >50 and <=75%, Q4: >75%.
Diabetic retinopathy presence will be based on their response from diabetic retionopathy question, categorized as 1 and 0.
We will used AIREADI data from 2023-2025, and will exclude participants whose age were less than 20 years old, who didn't say yes when asked about diabetes diagnosis, missing status on diabetic retinopathy, missing serum albumin (ALB) (g/dL), serum creatinine (SCR) (mg/dL), urinary albumin (ug/ml), urinary creatinine (ug/ml).
for analysis, include the following covariates: age.
