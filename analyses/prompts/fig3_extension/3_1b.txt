We plan to conduct a logistic regression analysis to examine the relationship between serum albumin (g/dL) and diabetic retinopathy using AIREADI data from the years 2023 to 2025.
The study will include all individuals whose age was 30 years or older who have complete data on both serum albumin (g/dL) and diabetic retinopathy status.
Participants will be excluded if their age is less than 30 years old, or age when first told the patient had diabetes was before age 30, don't have diabetes.
We will also exclude anyone with missing data on serum albumin or diabetic retinopathy status.
The main predictor in the analysis will be serum albumin (g/dL), categorized into quartiles Q1: <25%, Q2: ≥25 and <50, Q3: ≥50 and <75%, Q4: ≥75%. 
The outcome variable will be the presence or absence of diabetic retinopathy, coded as 1 for "Yes" and 0 for "No".
We will adjust the analysis for potential confounding factors including age.
