We want to do a logistic regression analysis using the exam survey weights to study the association between UACR-SACR ratio and diabetic retinopathy. 
UACR-SACR ratio  will be calculated as urinary albumin-to-creatinine ratio UACR (mg/g)/ (serum albumin ALB (g/dL)/serum creatinine SCR (mg/dL))) and then categorized in quartiles(%) Q1: <=25, Q2: >25 and <=50, Q3: >50 and <=75, Q4: >75. Diabetic retinopathy presence will be based on their response from diabetes affected eyes/had retinopathy, categorized as 1 and 0.
We will use NHANES data from 2009-2016, and will exclude participants whose age in years at screening were less than 20 years old, who had missing response when asked doctor told you have diabetes or didn't say yes when asked doctor told you have diabetes, missing status on diabetes affected eyes/had retinopathy, missing serum albumin (ALB)(g/dL), serum creatinine SCR (mg/dL), urinary albumin-to-creatinine ratio((UACR (mg/g)) 
for analysis, include the following covariates: age in years at screening, gender, race/Hispanic origin categorized as Mexican American, Other Hispanic, Non-Hispanic White, Non-Hispanic Black, Other, education level categorized as primary school, secondary school, college or above, not recorded, and poverty income ratio (PIR), categorized as Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4, and Q_unknown (for missing values).
