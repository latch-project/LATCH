We will plan to perform a weighted Cox regression for NHANES data from 2005-2018.
Participants will be excluded if have missing data on any of the following: fasting plasma glucose (mmol/L) or glycohemoglobin (HbA1c) (%) or months follow up examination or mortality status or age at screening was less than 20 years old.
They will also be excluded if they don't have diabetes or prediabetes when they are defined as the following:
diabetes defined as meeting any of these: glycohemoglobin (%)>=6.5 or fasting plasma glucose >=126 mg/dl, doctor told you have diabetes yes or taking inculin now yes.
prediabtes defined as meeting any of these: glycohemoglobin (%) >=5.7 less or equal to 6.4 fasting plasma glucose level between 100-125 mg/dl or yes to do you have prediabetes.
Main predictor is SHR - which is fasting plasma glucose (mmol/l)/ (1.59* glycohemoglobin (%)-2.59), categorized as quartiles, categorized in quartiles(%) Q1: <=25, Q2: >25 and <=50, Q3: >50 and <=75, Q4: >75.
The analysis will employ a weighted Cox proportional hazards regression model and covariates will include age at screening, gender, race/Hispanic origin.
The main outcome is all-cause mortality with months followup examination serving as the time-to-event variable.
