This study will perform a logistic regression using NHANES data from 2005-2008.
Exclude participants whose age at screening is less than 40 years old, and without diabetes when diabetes is defined as one of the following: glycated hemoglobin equal or more than 6.5%, fasting plasma glucose >= 7 mmol/l, oral glucose tolerace test >=11 mmol/l, self reported diagnosis of diabetes, current use of insulin, or current use of diabetes medication.
Exclude participant who are missing four level retinopathy severity, and monocyte count and lymphocyte count, and who did not have retinopathy based on the severity level.
Main variable of interest is (monocyte count/lymphocyte count)*10 and outcome is when four level retinopathy severity is proliferative retinopathy. Covariates are gender, race/Hispanic origin, and age at screening.
